Anna’s Word Scrambler
Anna wants to communicate with her friend George using secret messages so she decided to make a word scrambler. Her scrambler takes a word and prints the first letter, then the first two letters, then the first three letters and so on until the whole string is printed with each sequence on a new line.
Input: String str
Precondition: length of str is < 20
Output: Print the output to the console.
Sample:
Input: “scramble”
Output:
s
sc
scr
scra
scram
scarmb
scrambl
scramble
