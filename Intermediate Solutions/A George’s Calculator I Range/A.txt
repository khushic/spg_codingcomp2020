George’s Calculator I: Range
Oh no! George is having a tough time with online school so he decided to make a calculator to check his homework. One of the functions of his calculator is to find the range in a list of numbers (the range is the difference between the smallest and largest numbers). George isn’t very good at programming either, so help him figure out how to code it!
Input: Array of ints arr
Precondition: length of arr is < 100
Output: int
Sample:
Input: 1, 4, 6, 3, 2					Output: 5
